Woods

Most common types of woods:

Driver, 3 wood, and 5 wood


Description:

Woods are the longest club in the bag. Both literally and in the sense that they go the furthest. Woods used to be actually made out of wood but are now made of different metals.


Where to use them:

You would usually use them on the tee or in the fairway. It is harder to hit a wood from the rough or in tough lies.
