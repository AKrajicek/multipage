Irons

Most common types of irons:

1-9 iron


Description:

Irons are the most versatile clubs in the bag. It is easier to shape a shot with an iron than almost any other club. Irons also take up more of your bag than any other club, with most bags having at least 5-6 irons.


Where to use them:

You can use an Iron from pretty much anywhere. They are perfect in the fairways and rough. You can use them near the green if you go for a bump and run. You use them on the tee for most par 3's.
